Online Fuel Services



Our project is an online petrol ordering system that enables ease for the customers. It overcomes the disadvantages of the traditional queueing system. Our Project system is a medium to order fuel without any hassle from fuel stations. This system improves the method of taking the order from customer.

This system also provides a feedback system in which user can rate the fuel types. Also, the proposed system can recommend Fuel stations based on the distance so that the delivery time will be as low as possible. The online fuel ordering system sets up a fuel types online and customers can easily place the order as per their wish. Also with a fuel types, customers can easily track the orders.

The payment can be made online or pay-on-delivery . For more secured ordering separate accounts are maintained for each user by providing them an ID and a password.
